📱 Electronics Sales Transaction Analysis

An end-to-end data analytics and predictive modeling project that transforms one year of electronics sales data into actionable business recommendations to support Black Friday decision-making.

🔗 GitHub Repository 📊 View Presentation

Project Overview

As Black Friday approached, an electronics company sought data-driven insights to optimize inventory planning, target high-value customer segments, and improve promotional strategies.

Using transaction records from the previous fiscal year, this project combined exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, and predictive modeling to uncover customer behavior patterns and support strategic business decisions.

Business Questions

  • Which customer groups generate the most revenue?
  • Which products should receive additional inventory investment?
  • How do purchasing behaviors change over time?
  • Can customer actions be predicted to improve marketing effectiveness?

Dataset

  • 20,000 transaction records
  • 16 original variables
  • September 2023 – September 2024
  • Source: Kaggle

Key variables included customer demographics, product information, shipping preferences, payment methods, ratings, and transaction values.

Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering

  • Imputed missing gender values using probability-based assignment.
  • Replaced missing add-on purchases with "None".
  • Created a new revenue metric:

Final Price = Total Price + Add-on Total

Key Insights

  • Middle-aged customers (35–64) generated the highest revenue.
  • Male customers consistently spent more across age groups.
  • Smartphones were the most profitable product category.
  • Headphones generated the lowest revenue.
  • Sales peaked during the holiday season before declining mid-2024.
  • Most transactions received 3-star ratings.

Predictive Modeling Results

🚚 Overnight Shipping

83% predictive performance

Identified customers likely to select overnight shipping, enabling targeted shipping promotions.

⭐ Loyalty Membership

78% predictive performance

Predicted customers likely to join the loyalty program, supporting personalized incentives.

📅 Thursday Shopping

86–87% accuracy

Forecasted Thursday purchasing behavior to optimize promotions and inventory planning.

Business Recommendations

  • Prioritize middle-aged customer segments.
  • Increase smartphone inventory and promotions.
  • Use predictive models to personalize shipping offers.
  • Expand loyalty membership campaigns.
  • Investigate declining Thursday sales despite strong shopping likelihood.
